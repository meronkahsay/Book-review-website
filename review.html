About the author

Trevor Noah

Trevor Noah is a South African comedian, writer, producer, political commentator, actor, and television host. He was the host of The Daily Show, an American late-night talk show and satirical news program on Comedy Central, from 2015 to 2022. Noah has won various awards, including two Primetime Emmy Awards

Other Books

In Trevor Noah’s book ‘Born a Crime’, he narrates his stories from South Africa
pre and post-apartheid. Every chapter focuses on a specific theme from his child
hood in a humorous way including his relationship with his mum, racism, finding
his identity, domestic violence, family dynamics, and complexities of being raised
in a divided society.
